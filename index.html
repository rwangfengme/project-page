Dart Rem!

an Android application which allows users to customise reminders and alerts based on time or location.



REMINDER

Cutomized reminder helps you never miss any event/schedule. Our application supports event sharing and is able to synchronise all the schedules into cloud server.

LOCATION

By implementing the location tracking service, our application could remind users with their schedules and events when they arrives at their pre-defined locations.

ACTIVITY

What are you doing? Standing? Walking? Jogging? Our app knows. It can gives you some hint.



SYNC

Want to view your schedule anywhere? No problem, with the help of Google App Engine and our user system, you can retrieve or update your shcedule with the cloud.

SHARE

Reminding your children to put more clothes on in a cold day, you can create a reminder for them. Shared reminders will shown in your friends' schedule list, as a mutual reminder.
